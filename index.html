Introducing Biome

A platform for organizing and finding internal information using a unified search interface. Empower your teams to search for data from any datasource with simply their browser's search bar in order to work smarter, not harder. Biome is aimed to solve the problems knowledge workers currently face:

Inability to self-serve

Poor search leads to: Tapping coworkers, ineffective meetings, or the recreation of information

Too many apps

A typical knowledge worker utilizes roughly 15 apps as part of their stack at work

Disaggregated Information

Leads to knowledge slows and impacts our ability to move work forward

Unmanagable data silos

Employee data footprints continue to grow and it is only getting bigger

Discovery via unified search

Biome creates a unifying search experience across all your data sources. Use your browser's search bar to find any of your business's resources. No extra apps or software needed.

Searching for your company's information is fast and easy. Leveraging your existing internet browser, you are able to quickly search through your different business resources and have everything in one place.

Power of customization

Create custom search terms which will redirect to apps and documents.

Redirect to third-party apps with SSO to create a seemless and productive experience.

One-click onboarding

No need for multiple logins to access many company resources anymore. With one-click onboarding you just sign into your apps once and have acess from now on via SSO.

Having one single login per user allows your team to search and utilize all resources and apps with ease - helping them reach their full potential by increasing productivity.
